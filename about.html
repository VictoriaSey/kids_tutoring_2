Our Mission

The Khepri Eduprep Story

Founded in 2009 by Mr. Asamoah, a veteran educator with over 15 years of experience, Khepri Eduprep was born from a simple idea: every student deserves a chance to shine. We noticed that the traditional classroom setting couldn't always cater to individual learning styles. We set out to create a supportive, personalized tutoring environment where students can thrive.

Today, we've helped hundreds of junior and senior high school students not just pass their exams, but develop a genuine passion for learning that lasts a lifetime.
